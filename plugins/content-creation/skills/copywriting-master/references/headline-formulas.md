# Headline Formulas Reference

## 50+ Proven Headline Templates

### Result-Focused Headlines

1. How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]
   Example: How to Double Your Email List in 30 Days

2. The [number]-step system for [desired outcome]
   Example: The 5-Step System for Writing Landing Pages That Convert

3. Get [specific result] without [painful thing]
   Example: Get 10K Followers Without Posting Every Day

4. How [audience] [achieved result] in [timeframe]
   Example: How 47 Agencies Hit $1M ARR in Under 12 Months

5. [Number] ways to [achieve benefit]
   Example: 7 Ways to Cut Your Ad Spend While Increasing Conversions

6. The complete guide to [topic]
   Example: The Complete Guide to Zero-Downtime Deployments

7. From [starting point] to [end result] in [timeframe]
   Example: From Zero to 100K Subscribers in 6 Months

## Headline Writing Guidelines

### Structure
- Lead with the benefit
- Include specific numbers when possible
- Address the target audience
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Keep under 10-12 words when possible

### Power Words to Include
Urgency: Now, Today, Limited, Fast, Quick
Value: Free, Save, Bonus, New, Proven
Trust: Guaranteed, Certified, Expert, Tested
Results: Transform, Achieve, Unlock, Discover

### Words to Avoid
- Revolutionary
- Cutting-edge
- Synergy
- Leverage
- World-class
- Best-in-class
- Next-generation

### Testing Framework
Always create 5-10 variations and test:
1. Different angles (benefit vs curiosity vs social proof)
2. Different specificity levels
3. Question vs statement
4. With/without numbers
5. Short vs long versions
