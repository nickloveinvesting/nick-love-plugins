## B2B Copywriting Formulas for Carousels

Four proven formulas for structuring carousel content that converts to high-ticket leads. Each uses a different psychological angle.

### Formula #1: Symptom vs. Root Cause

**Psychology:** Surface-level solutions don't work because people are treating symptoms. Revealing the root cause positions you as the only one who truly understands.

**Structure:**
- Slides 1-2: Identify surface symptoms the audience sees
- Slides 3-4: Reveal the root cause they're missing
- Slides 5-7: Show the transformation/solution
- Slides 8-9: Proof/case study
- Slide 10: CTA to masterclass

**Example:**
- Slide 1: "Your team keeps quitting... but hiring more people won't fix it"
- Slide 2: "The SYMPTOM: High turnover, low engagement, constant firefighting"
- Slide 3: "The ROOT CAUSE: You're managing tasks, not leading vision"
- Slides 4-6: "The 3-pillar leadership framework that eliminates 80% of turnover"
- Slides 7-8: "How Sarah went from 40% annual turnover to 8%"
- Slide 9: "The full framework inside our free masterclass"
- Slide 10: "Comment 'LEAD' to get instant access"

### Formula #2: Enemy/Hero Framework

**Psychology:** Unite the audience against a common enemy while positioning yourself as the guide. Creates strong emotional bonding and positions you on their side, not as the salesperson.

**Core Principle:** The customer is the hero. The problem is the enemy. You are the guide.

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Introduce the enemy (system, belief, competitor)
- Slides 2-3: Show how the enemy is hurting them
- Slide 4: Position yourself on their team
- Slides 5-7: Reveal the plan to defeat the enemy
- Slides 8-9: Show the victory (case study/results)
- Slide 10: CTA with urgency

**Enemy Examples:**
- "The Growth-at-All-Costs Myth (that's burning you out)"
- "The Hiring Treadmill (why you can't find 'good people')"
- "The Revenue Ceiling (hidden in your current business model)"
- "The Time Trap (disguised as 'being hands-on')"

**Example:**
- Slide 1: "The enemy isn't your team. It's the system you inherited."
- Slide 2: "You were taught: 'Work harder. Be present. Control everything.'"
- Slide 3: "Result? You're the bottleneck. Your team can't grow. You can't scale."
- Slide 4: "I used to believe it too. Until I built a $5M business working 25 hours/week."
- Slides 5-7: "The 3-phase delegation system that eliminates you as the bottleneck"
- Slides 8-9: "How Marcus scaled from $800K to $2.3M while taking 8 weeks off last year"
- Slide 10: "Join 847 owners who've defeated the system → Comment 'FREEDOM'"

### Formula #3: The Myth Buster

**Psychology:** Challenging common beliefs sparks curiosity, triggers debate in comments, and positions you as an authority who sees what others don't.

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: "MYTH: [Widely held belief]"
- Slide 2: "Why everyone believes this (and why it's killing your growth)"
- Slides 3-5: "TRUTH: What actually works (backed by data)"
- Slides 6-8: "The 3-step system that replaces the myth"
- Slide 9: Mini case study
- Slide 10: CTA

**High-Converting Myth Examples:**
- "MYTH: You need to post daily to grow on social"
- "MYTH: Hiring more people solves revenue plateaus"
- "MYTH: A big team means a big business"
- "MYTH: You need to work 60+ hours to hit $1M+"

### Formula #4: Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS)

**Psychology:** Intensifies pain before presenting the solution as relief. Loss aversion makes this the highest-urgency formula.

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Identify specific problem
- Slides 2-4: Agitate (make it worse, show cascading consequences)
- Slides 5-7: Present the solution framework
- Slides 8-9: Proof/results
- Slide 10: CTA

**Example:**
- Slide 1: "You're 6 months away from losing your best clients"
- Slide 2: "They're not leaving because of price. They're leaving because you're invisible."
- Slide 3: "While you're 'too busy' for marketing... competitors are building trust daily"
- Slide 4: "The cost: $847K in lost revenue over 18 months (based on average client LTV)"
- Slides 5-7: "The Authority Engine: 20 minutes/week to become the obvious choice"
- Slides 8-9: "David went from 3 inbound leads/month to 47"
- Slide 10: "Full system revealed → Comment 'AUTHORITY'"

## 5 B2B Hook Templates

Fill-in-the-blank templates specifically for B2B and high-ticket coaching carousels. Business owners respond better to pain-point hooks than flashy ones.

**Template #1: The Bold Claim**
```
"You're losing [SPECIFIC $$$] to [SPECIFIC PROBLEM] (even if you don't see it yet)"
```
- "You're losing $60K to team inefficiency (even if you don't see it yet)"
- "You're bleeding $200K in hidden revenue leaks (your P&L won't show this)"

**Template #2: The Provocative Question**
```
"Are you making this [$REVENUE] mistake that keeps you stuck at [PLATEAU]?"
```
- "Are you making this $1M mistake that keeps you stuck at $800K?"
- "Losing clients because your marketing is silent?"

**Template #3: The Contrarian Stat**
```
"[SHOCKING %] of [TARGET] fail at [GOAL] because they [COMMON MISTAKE]"
```
- "87% of 7-figure owners still work 50+ hours because they optimize the wrong metrics"
- "90% of B2B buyers start with a search. You're not showing up."

**Template #4: The Time/Money Promise**
```
"How to [DESIRED OUTCOME] in [TIMEFRAME] without [COMMON SACRIFICE]"
```
- "How to scale to $2M in 9 months without hiring 20 people"
- "How to double profit margins in 60 days without cutting costs"

**Template #5: The Enemy Identification**
```
"The real reason you're stuck isn't [OBVIOUS ANSWER]... it's [HIDDEN ENEMY]"
```
- "The real reason you can't scale isn't your team... it's your operating system"
- "The real reason you're burning out isn't workload... it's decision architecture"

## Comment-to-DM Setup

**Tool:** ManyChat (Meta-approved platform)

**Core Features Needed:**
- Comment-to-DM automation
- Keyword triggers
- Quick replies/buttons
- Email capture integration
- Recurring notifications (for follow-up beyond 24 hours)

### Setup Workflow

**Step 1: Create Trigger**
- Platform: Instagram
- Trigger type: "Instagram comment on a post"
- Select specific carousel post
- Add filter: Exact match for keyword (e.g., "FREEDOM", "SCALE", "PROFIT")
- CRITICAL: Check "Track first-level comments only" — prevents automation firing multiple times in threads

**Step 2: Design CTA on Final Slide**
- Clear instruction in caption: "Comment [KEYWORD] below to get instant access"
- Keep trigger words simple: 1-2 words maximum
- Contrasting colors on final slide to draw eye
- Arrow or visual cue pointing to comment section

**Step 3: Keyword Strategy**
Use different keywords for different carousels to track performance:
- "FREEDOM" for delegation carousel
- "PROFIT" for margin carousel
- "SCALE" for growth carousel
- "SYSTEM" for operations carousel

This lets you measure which content topic + hook combination drives the highest comment-to-lead conversion.

## The 5-Step DM Sequence (58%+ Comment-to-Email Conversion)

### Message #1: Instant Reply (Within Seconds of Comment)

**Purpose:** Deliver value immediately, ask qualifying question

```
Hey [Name]! 🎯

Here's your instant access to the [FRAMEWORK NAME] Masterclass:
[LINK]

Quick question before you dive in —
What's your #1 challenge with [TOPIC] right now?

(This helps me customize the training for you)
```

- Instant gratification (link delivered immediately)
- Begins qualification
- Personalizes experience
- 90%+ open rate within minutes

### Message #2: Smart Follow-Up (4 Hours Later — If No Reply)

**Purpose:** Recover non-responders, create urgency

```
Hey [Name] —

Just making sure you got the masterclass link!

Don't forget to grab your spot before we hit capacity
(we cap at [NUMBER] live attendees)

Reply with any questions 👇
```

This single follow-up increases conversion 15-20%.

### Message #3: Name Capture (After They Reply)

**Purpose:** Build relationship, enable email collection

```
Perfect! Love that you're focused on [THEIR ANSWER].

By the way, what's your first name?
I want to make sure the system sends you the right pre-work 📧
```

Required before most email integrations will work.

### Message #4: Email Collection + Calendar Link

**Purpose:** Move lead to owned channel (email), secure registration

```
Thanks [Name]!

Drop your best email below and I'll send you:

✓ Masterclass calendar invite
✓ Pre-work guide (5-min read)
✓ The [SPECIFIC TOOL] template

(This way you get reminders + can prepare for maximum value)
```

Connect ManyChat to email platform (Kartra, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign). Tag subscriber with campaign name. Trigger automated email sequence.

### Message #5: Engagement Check (23 Hours After Initial DM)

**Purpose:** Maintain conversation, increase show-up rate

```
Hey [Name] —

Did you get a chance to check out the pre-work?

What section resonated most with you?

(Knowing this helps me go deep on what matters to YOU on the live call)
```

Engaged leads show up at 40-50% vs. 25-30% for non-engaged.

## Advanced: Beyond the 24-Hour Window

Instagram restricts automated DMs to 24 hours after user interaction. To nurture leads beyond this:

**Solution: ManyChat Recurring Notifications**
- Send promotional messages to opted-in users beyond 24 hours
- Broadcast reminders about upcoming masterclass
- Share additional value content pre-event

**Performance Data:**
- 100% read rate via Recurring Notifications
- 75% click-through rate on automated DMs
- 491% ROI in documented case studies

### Pre-Masterclass Sequence (Email)
Once email is captured, trigger a 3-5 email sequence:
1. Calendar invite + pre-work guide
2. "What to expect" value-building email (24 hours before)
3. Day-of reminder (morning + 1 hour before)
4. Post-event replay for no-shows
5. Follow-up for attendees who didn't convert

## CTA Slide Design for Conversion

### Must-Have Elements on Slide 10

1. **Large, bold heading:** "Want the Full Framework?"
2. **Benefit bullets:** 3-4 specific outcomes from masterclass
3. **Clear instruction:** "👇 Comment [KEYWORD] Below 👇"
4. **Social proof:** "Join 847 owners who've already implemented this"
5. **Urgency/scarcity (optional):** "Next live session: 72 hours"

### CTA Slide Design Rules

- Contrasting background color vs. rest of carousel (makes it "feel" different)
- Directional cues: arrows pointing down to comment area
- White space: don't clutter — single clear action
- Consistent branding: same fonts/colors as previous slides

### High-Converting CTA Templates

```
"The full 5-part framework (including the [SPECIFIC TOOL/TEMPLATE])
is inside our 90-minute masterclass.

Comment '[KEYWORD]' and I'll send you instant access 👇"
```

```
"Inside the masterclass, you'll get:
✓ The exact [FRAMEWORK NAME] template
✓ Live walkthrough with [SPECIFIC CASE STUDY]
✓ Q&A to customize for your business

Comment '[KEYWORD]' to join [XXX] owners who've already implemented this"
```

### Psychological Triggers in CTA

- **Social proof:** "Join 847 owners who've already..."
- **Specificity:** "90-minute masterclass" not "free training"
- **Curiosity gap:** "The template we can't show publicly"
- **Low friction:** "Comment [WORD]" not "Fill out a form"

### Caption Strategy

The Instagram caption should:
- Amplify the message from slides with personal story/context
- Ask a direct question related to carousel topic
- Include the CTA again: "Drop a comment with [KEYWORD] and I'll send you instant access"
- 3-5 strategic niche hashtags (not 30 generic ones)

## A/B Testing Framework

### Variables to Test

1. **Trigger words:** "FREE" vs. "SCALE" vs. "PROFIT" — track which converts better
2. **DM sequence timing:** 4-hour delay vs. 8-hour delay vs. 24-hour delay
3. **Email collection placement:** Message #3 vs. Message #4
4. **Masterclass title:** "The [X] Framework" vs. "The [X] Blueprint" vs. "The [X] Masterclass"
5. **Show-up incentives:** Pre-work guide vs. PDF template vs. exclusive bonus
6. **Hook type:** Test two different hooks on the same carousel content one week apart

### A/B Testing Protocol

- Change one variable at a time
- Run each variant for minimum 7 days
- Track the specific metric that variable impacts
- Minimum 100 comments per variant before drawing conclusions
- Document results and double down on winner

## Implementation Roadmap

### Week 1: Foundation
- Set up ManyChat account, connect Instagram Business profile
- Create first carousel using Symptom vs. Root Cause or Enemy/Hero formula
- Write DM sequence (Messages #1-5)
- Test automation with dummy comments

### Week 2: Launch
- Post carousel, monitor comment triggers
- Manually engage with first 20 commenters to refine sequence
- Track metrics: comment rate, DM open rate, email conversion rate
- Fix any automation issues in real-time

### Week 3-4: Scale
- Create 2-3 additional carousels using different formulas
- A/B test different hooks and trigger words
- Analyze which formula/hook combination drives highest comment rates
- Double down on top performer

### Week 5+: Optimize
- Build automated follow-up sequences for non-attendees
- Create replay funnel for masterclass no-shows
- Implement Recurring Notifications for ongoing engagement
- Scale winning carousel with paid promotion

### Critical Success Factors
- **Speed:** DM response time under 60 seconds
- **Simplicity:** One clear CTA per carousel
- **Personalization:** Use name, reference their specific pain point
- **Value-first:** Deliver masterclass link immediately, qualify second
- **Consistency:** Post 2-3 carousels per week, rotate formulas
