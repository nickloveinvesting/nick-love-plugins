# Testimonial Templates Reference

## Testimonial Formula

The best testimonials follow this structure:

Before then After then Because

Before [situation]. After [specific result]. Because [what made the difference].

## Types of Testimonials

### 1. Results-Based Testimonials
Focus on specific, measurable outcomes.

Example:
We increased our conversion rate from 2% to 8% in just 30 days. 
Our lead costs dropped by 60%.
- Sarah M., Marketing Director at TechCorp

### 2. Transformation Testimonials
Show the journey from before to after.

Example:
Before [Product], I was spending 15 hours a week on content creation. 
Now I spend 3 hours and get better results. It completely changed how I work.
- Mike R., Freelance Consultant

### 3. Emotional Testimonials
Capture the feeling, not just the facts.

Example:
For the first time in years, I actually feel confident about my marketing. 
No more guessing. No more stress. Just clarity.
- Jennifer L., Small Business Owner

### 4. Credibility Testimonials
From recognizable names or companies.

Example:
This is the system we use across all our portfolio companies. 
It just works.
- David Chen, Partner at Sequoia Capital

### 5. Objection-Handling Testimonials
Address specific concerns through customer stories.

Example:
I was skeptical at first - I had tried similar tools before. 
But this was different. The onboarding took 10 minutes, 
and I saw results by day 3.
- Chris T., Agency Owner

## Testimonial Collection Templates

### Email Request Template

Subject: Quick favor? (2 min)

Hi [Name],

I noticed you have been using [Product] for [X months] now, 
and I would love to hear how it is going.

Would you mind sharing a quick testimonial? 

Just answer these three questions:
1. What was your situation before using [Product]?
2. What specific results have you seen?
3. What would you tell someone considering [Product]?

Even 2-3 sentences would be incredibly helpful.

Thanks so much!
[Your name]

### Survey Questions for Testimonials

1. What was your biggest challenge before using [Product]?
2. What specific results have you achieved?
3. How has [Product] changed your [work/life/business]?
4. What would you tell someone who is on the fence?
5. What surprised you most about [Product]?

## Testimonial Enhancement Tips

### Add Specificity

Bad: Great product!
Good: Increased our conversions by 340% in Q3.

Bad: Really helpful
Good: Saved me 12 hours per week on content planning.

Bad: Highly recommend
Good: Best investment we made this year. ROI was 5x in month one.

### Add Context

Bad: - John
Good: - John Martinez, CEO at ScaleUp (50 employees)

Bad: - Happy customer
Good: - Sarah Chen, Marketing Director at TechCorp | Using [Product] for 8 months

### Add Visual Proof
- Headshot photos
- Company logos
- Screenshots of results
- Before/after visuals
- Video testimonials

## Where to Place Testimonials

### Homepage
- Social proof bar near hero (numbers + quick quotes)
- Dedicated testimonials section (3-6 detailed ones)
- Near CTAs (risk-reducing quotes)

### Landing Pages
- After stating benefits (proof they work)
- Near pricing (value validation)
- Before final CTA (last push)

### Product Pages
- Feature-specific testimonials
- Use case testimonials
- Industry-specific testimonials

### Checkout/Signup
- Short, reassuring quotes
- Focus on ease/speed
- Address last-minute objections

## Testimonial Quality Checklist

- Includes specific numbers or results
- Mentions the transformation (before/after)
- Feels authentic (not overly polished)
- Includes full name and title
- Relevant to the page/offer
- Addresses potential objections
- Diverse representation of customers
- Has photo or video if possible

## Legal Considerations

### Best Practices
- Get written permission to use testimonials
- Do not edit quotes without approval
- Use real names when possible (with permission)
- Include disclaimers for income/results claims
- Keep records of all testimonials and permissions

### Disclaimer Example
Results vary. These testimonials represent individual 
experiences and are not guaranteed for all users.
